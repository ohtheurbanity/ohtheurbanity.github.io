Patterns in Pulaski

Pulaski County, Arkansas has experienced major shifts in demographics and income from 1970 - 2010

2.3.2020

Using the Longitudinal Tract Database (LTDB), a database which standardizes census data from 1970-2010 using 2010 U.S. Census tract geographies, changes in household income in Pulaski County, Arkansas were mapped over time. The LTDB data was cleaned using the Pandas library for Python and joined to U.S. Census tract geographies using "geopandas". The outputs were then webmapped using Leaflet.js.

Pulaski County, AR Percent White (1970-2010)

A large amount of "white flight" occurred in Pulaski County from 1970 to 1990. Since 1990, the county has become less segregated, although geographic differences in racial distribution continue to persist.

Pulaski County, AR Median Household Income (1970-2010)

Despite some integration over the past 30 years, large geographic disparities in household income continue to persist. Much like the rest of the United States, the standard deviation of household income, which can be seen in this context as a measure of geographic income inequality, has increased, while the inflation-adjusted average median houshold income has remained constant.
